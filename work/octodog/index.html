Octodog Photography

An online portfolio for an abstract photographer

Octodog Photography is the online portfolio of Charlotte Duckworth, a photographer with a keen eye for detail who has her own unique style of photography.

Based in the South of the UK, Charlotte does a wide variety of photography work and her main presence is on DeviantArt, where all of her different styles of unique work can be found.

The website is used to showcase a cross section of her work and skills for any potential clients or employers.

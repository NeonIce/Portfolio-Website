Whisper of Wolf

A redesign for an Artists portfolio

Whisper of Wolf is the online portfolio for Sam Clements, an artists with a passion for digital media, creating plush toys as well as full and partial costumes.

This was a redesign of her current website using a new framework and UI to showcase a selection of her work that included her art, creative works, plushies, costumes and more.

The site also has links to her presences on social media websites and has a contact email if people would like to commission her for work.

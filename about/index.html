Hi, I'm Michael. I'm a web developer from the UK.

I'm a web developer, who also does a bit of design, based in Bath and working for a company in Bristol.

I have over 2 years of commercial experience and over 5 years of freelance experience and in that time I've worked on a number of projects for a variety of clients, varying in style and complexity.

I have a passion for websites and love to design and develop them as a way of expressing my creativity - sometimes the simplest things can have the biggest impact.

I am completely self taught, learning web development the hard way - by trial and error to see what things do. However I pick things up quickly and I try to discover new ways to develop and to improve my skill.

Along with building websites I have a keen interest in app development and currently have some concepts in mind for a couple of apps. I also enjoy designing logos and icons.
